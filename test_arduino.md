First test (no shield):  
connect your arduino and mouse to 2 seperate usb ports on your computer  
Flash Mouse.ino to arduino  
run test_arduino.py, enter coordinates and then 0 or 1 to select to click or not
see if the arduino moves the mouse for you and if its able to click

First test (with shield):  
connect your arduino 1 1 usb port on your pc, connect your mouse to your arduino shield
Flash MouseShield.ino to arduino
Check if you can move the real mouse through the arduino
run test_arduino.py, enter coordinates and then 0 or 1 to select to click or not
see if the arduino moves the mouse for you and if its able to click
